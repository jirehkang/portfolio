# UX writing

Kim & Chang | February 2020

## Overview

As the content editor at Kim & Chang, Korea's leading law firm, I led website content strategy and UX writing for a full digital revamp. The goal was to create an online experience that reflected the firm's authority, expertise, and trustworthiness.

## Scope of work

- Wrote and edited copy for key pages, including service descriptions, attorney bios, and navigation menus, ensuring clarity, accessibility, and strategic positioning.
- Localized select content from Korean source material, adapting tone and language to resonate with a global audience while preserving the intent and nuance of the original.

## Outcome

- Delivered a seamless content experience tailored to global prospective partners.
- Helped position Kim & Chang as a trusted legal advisor on the global stage through clear, consistent, and brand-aligned language.
